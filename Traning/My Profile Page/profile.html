Futsal is a fast-paced, indoor version of soccer, played on a smaller, hard court with five players on each side. Originating in Uruguay in the 1930s, futsal emphasizes skill, control, and quick decision-making due to the limited space and fewer players. Unlike traditional soccer, futsal uses a smaller, harder ball, which encourages players to develop their dribbling, passing, and ball-handling abilities.
